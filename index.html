Our Motto

Assalamualaikum

Welcome to the National General Knowledge Olympiad,established in 17th of June,2020. The MOTTO of the organisation is "Learn_GK_explore_world". Knowledge holds a huge gesture to know the world,learn the world and explore the world. NGKO believes "An investment in Knowledge pays the best interest"-Benjamin Franklin. Everything has its limit in this globe but general knowledge as there is no ending of learning.
NGKO is conducted to encourage students to expose their talent of knowledge by testing through Olympiads based on current affairs. These exams will help in sharping and shaping a student's knowledge.

National General Knowledge Olympiad

Education is what keeps us going forward. Everything has a limit but in education, there is no limit.
